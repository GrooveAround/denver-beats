This week delivers everything from the rising pop-star energy of Kesha reclaiming her freedom at Red Rocks to the psychedelic dual-headliner fest Outside Days taking over Auraria Campus with My Morning Jacket and Cage The Elephant on consecutive days. Morgan Wallen pulls stadium duty at Empower Field while underground hip-hop and house beats dominate the club circuit.

Show of the Week

Outside Days

Auraria Campus · Saturday, May 30 & Sunday, May 31 · 12:00 PM

Two days, two completely different vibes: Saturday brings My Morning Jacket's arena-ready psych-rock alongside The Flaming Lips' cosmic chaos, while Sunday counters with Cage The Elephant and Tash Sultana's genre-fluid brilliance. This is Denver's answer to the boutique festival model—hometown accessibility with headliner firepower that rivals any major fest lineup.
